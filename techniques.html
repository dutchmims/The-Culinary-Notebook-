Essential Cooking Techniques

Mastering fundamental cooking techniques is key to becoming a great home cook. Below are some of the most important methods to improve your skills.

Sautéing

Cooking food quickly in a small amount of oil over medium-high heat. This method enhances flavors and creates a rich texture.

Boiling & Simmering

Boiling involves cooking food at a high temperature in water or broth, while simmering is a gentler process at lower temperatures.

Grilling

Using direct heat to cook food, grilling enhances flavors through caramelization and charring.

Baking

Cooking food with dry heat in an oven, commonly used for bread, pastries, and roasted dishes.

Steaming

Cooking food by exposing it to steam, a healthy method that preserves nutrients and natural flavors.

Roasting

Cooking food slowly in an oven at high heat to develop deep flavors and crispy textures.
