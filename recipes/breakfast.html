Delicious Breakfast Recipes

Start your day with these easy and nutritious breakfast ideas!

Classic Pancakes

Ingredients: Flour, milk, eggs, sugar, baking powder, butter.

Instructions: Mix ingredients, cook on a skillet, and serve with syrup.

Avocado Toast

Ingredients: Bread, avocado, eggs, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Toast bread, mash avocado, top with egg and seasoning.

Oatmeal with Berries

Ingredients: Oats, milk, honey, mixed berries.

Instructions: Cook oats, top with honey and berries, and enjoy.
