Delicious Dinner Recipes

Enjoy a hearty and flavorful dinner with these easy-to-make recipes!

Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients: Ground beef, spaghetti, tomatoes, garlic, onion, basil, olive oil.

Instructions: Brown beef with garlic and onions, add tomatoes, simmer, and serve over cooked spaghetti.

Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients: Chicken breast, pasta, heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, garlic, butter.

Instructions: Cook chicken, prepare Alfredo sauce with cream and Parmesan, toss with pasta and serve.

Vegetarian Tacos

Ingredients: Tortillas, black beans, corn, avocado, lettuce, salsa.

Instructions: Fill tortillas with beans, corn, avocado, lettuce, and salsa. Serve with a squeeze of lime.
