Decadent Dessert Recipes

Indulge in these sweet treats for the perfect ending to any meal!

Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients: Dark chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, flour, vanilla.

Instructions: Melt chocolate and butter, mix with sugar, eggs, and flour, then bake until the center is gooey.

Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients: Cream cheese, graham cracker crust, strawberries, sugar, eggs.

Instructions: Make the crust, blend cream cheese filling, bake, and top with fresh strawberries.

Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, eggs, butter, vanilla extract, baking powder.

Instructions: Mix ingredients, bake in cupcake tins, and frost with buttercream frosting.
