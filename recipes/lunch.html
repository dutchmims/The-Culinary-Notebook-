Easy & Tasty Lunch Recipes

Enjoy a delicious and satisfying lunch with these easy-to-make recipes!

Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients: Grilled chicken breast, lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, feta cheese, olive oil.

Instructions: Chop ingredients, mix in a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and enjoy!

Caprese Sandwich

Ingredients: Ciabatta bread, fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, basil, balsamic glaze.

Instructions: Layer ingredients on the bread, drizzle with balsamic glaze, and serve.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients: Bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, soy sauce, sesame oil.

Instructions: Stir-fry vegetables in sesame oil, add soy sauce, and serve with rice.
